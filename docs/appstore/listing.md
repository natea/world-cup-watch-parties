## Description

The 2026 FIFA World Cup is coming to Massachusetts — with seven matches at Gillette Stadium and hundreds of ways to watch across the state. WorldCup Watcher puts all of it in one place.

Find your match, your crowd, and your spot:

• SCHEDULE — every match, grouped by day, in your local time. Hide games that are already over with one tap.

• MAP — see watch parties near you. Enter a ZIP or address (or use your location) and sort venues by distance.

• BY TEAM — follow a country and see both where it's playing and which bars are its supporter hubs (Scotland's Tartan Army HQ, the USA outpost, France's home bar, and more).

Filter the way you actually decide:
• Family-friendly (all-ages, and we hide venues that turn 21+ later in the evening)
• Free vs. ticketed
• Indoor vs. outdoor
• Venue type — bar, brewery, plaza/fan fest, park, hotel, community space, and more
• Region — Greater Boston, Cambridge/Somerville, North Shore, South Shore, MetroWest, Foxborough, Worcester

From the FIFA Fan Festival on City Hall Plaza to free municipal watch parties, soccer-bar supporter crowds, brewery and waterfront screenings — WorldCup Watcher helps you find the right place to be for every game.

A finder, not a ticketing site. We link out; we don't transact.
