# Privacy Policy — WorldCup Watcher

_Last updated: June 18, 2026_

WorldCup Watcher ("the app") helps you find World Cup 2026 watch parties across Massachusetts. We designed it to collect as little as possible. This policy explains what the app does and does not collect.

## The short version

- **No account.** You don't sign in. We don't ask for your name, email, or phone number.
- **No tracking.** We don't track you across apps or websites, and we don't sell or share personal data.
- **Location is optional and not stored.** If you tap "use my location," your coordinates are used once to sort nearby venues, then discarded.

## Location

The app offers a proximity search: enter a ZIP code or address, or tap **use my location**.

- If you choose "use my location," the app reads your device's current location (with your permission) and sends it to our server **only** to calculate distances to venues and order them. It is **not stored**, **not logged with your identity**, and **not used for advertising or tracking**.
- The feature is entirely optional. The app is fully usable by typing a ZIP code or address instead, and works fine if you deny location permission.

## Analytics

We use privacy-friendly, aggregate analytics to understand which features are used (for example, how many people open the map). This analytics:

- does not use cookies or cross-site/cross-app identifiers,
- is not linked to your identity,
- is not used to track you.

## Information we do NOT collect

- No accounts, names, emails, or phone numbers.
- No contacts, photos, calendar, or microphone access.
- No advertising identifiers; no third-party ad networks.
- No purchases or payment data (the app does not sell tickets; it links out to venues).

## External links

Venue listings link to third-party sites (e.g., Google Maps, venue websites). Those sites have their own privacy practices, which we don't control.

## Children

The app is rated for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
