Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

Match Day MA: Soccer 2026 helps you find 2026 tournament watch parties across Massachusetts, as a website and as an iOS app. It is operated by Jazkarta, Inc. d/b/a StageHopper (www.jazkarta.com), referred to as “we” / “us” below. We built it to collect as little as possible. We do not sell your data and we do not track you across other apps or websites.

Information we collect

  • Location (optional): If you tap “use my location” for the proximity search, we read your device location while you are using the app to sort watch parties by distance. It is used once to compute distances and is not stored on our servers and not shared. The feature is entirely optional — you can instead type a ZIP code or address, and the app works fine if you deny location access.
  • Usage analytics: We use Umami to record anonymous, aggregated page/screen views so we can see which features are used. Umami uses no cookies, sets no cross-site identifiers, is not linked to your identity, and is not used to track you. This is the only analytics we run.
  • Map tiles: Map tiles on the map view are served by OpenStreetMap, which processes your IP address and map interactions to deliver tiles, per their policies.

Match Day MA: Soccer 2026 has no accounts and no login, so we do not collect names, emails, or passwords. We do not use PostHog, Datadog, Mapbox, advertising identifiers, or any third-party ad networks. Venue listings link out to third-party sites (for example Google Maps and venue websites); those sites have their own privacy policies.

How we use it

  • To show watch parties on a schedule, a map, and by team.
  • To sort nearby venues by distance when you ask.
  • To understand, in aggregate, which features are used.

Your choices

  • Location: optional — granted only when you tap “use my location,” and revocable any time in your device or browser settings.
  • Analytics: anonymous and cookie-free; it honors your browser/device Do-Not-Track / tracking settings.

Data retention & deletion

We don't hold personal accounts or profiles, and we don't store your location. Aggregated, anonymous analytics are retained by Umami per its standard retention. To request deletion of any data associated with you, contact us below.

Children

Match Day MA: Soccer 2026 is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13.
